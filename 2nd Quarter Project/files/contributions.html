Why Support the WFP and their Advocacies

Ever since its foundation in 1961, the World Food Programme has constantly been involved in addressing global social issues primarily of hunger, conflict, natural disasters, and other global emergencies. From its first emergency operation in Iran during the September 1962 earthquake, and up to the present, the WFP has always been committed in fulfilling their mission of saving lives and changing lives. In which they have done over hundreds of millions of individuals across over a hundred countries worldwide.

However, in order to execute and fund all its missions and programs globally, the WFP relies 100% on the kind donations that it receives from contributors spread all over the world. These include government assistance, corporate donors, and kind-hearted individuals. Accordingly, the WFP makes it their responsibility to guarantee donors that their donations will go to good cause, and simultaneously ensure that these donations and funds, including yours in the case that you have donated, are used wisely, efficiently and transparently.

By contributing, you are helping in the global fight against hunger, as we ensure that your support will come a long way in feeding the hungry. By donating, you are also helping in the WFP's operations in global emergencies caused by disasters and conflict. Your donations will also help provide financial support to those in desperate need, as well as help build sustainable and resilient communities, and in the WFP's campaigns that advocate for gender equality and action against climate change.

The WFP has been and is always prepared to respond to the call of service, and contributing your part will greatly help in the funding and execution of their missions around the world.

How You Can Contribute

Since the World Food Programme entirely relies on voluntary contributions, the organization actively encourages and warmly welcomes the international community and their contributions that aim to help WFP in their campaigns and operations worldwide. If by any means, you are also willing to contribute to the WFP's causes in saving lives and changing lives, there are dozens of methods you can do so. Listed below are some of the ways in which you can contribute to the organization.

Inform

Help inform others about the WFP, their work, and their advocacies, as well as inform what others can also do to help contribute to the organization and such causes and advocacies.

Participate

Participate in campaigns and activities promoted and organized by the WFP. Devise and create your own practices and activities that you believe can help end hunger and potentially save and change lives.

Donate

The WFP will gladly accept your cash and in-kind donations made both physically and virtually. Clicking the donate button beside will redirect you to the WFP's online donation page.

Volunteer

Use your skills and talents by volunteering and working in WFP missions and operations in your area. To see which job vacancies are available for application at WFP, kindly click the volunteer button beside.

Through contributing towards the WFP and its causes, you will be able to help in the global campaign of saving lives and changing lives by ending hunger and addressing similar issues, both on a local and global scale. With this, The WFP ensures that your contributions to the organizations are managed properly and will come a long way.
