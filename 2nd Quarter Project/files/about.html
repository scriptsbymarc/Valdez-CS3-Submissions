About the Website Author

Dyll Marc P. Valdez is currently a student at the Philippine Science High School - Southern Mindanao Campus. He was born in the City of Kidapawan, Philippines on August 11, 2008 (currently 14 years old). He finished elementary at the Notre Dame of Kidapawan College - IBED, and is now continuing his studies in Davao City, Philippines.

Dyll created this website in support of the World Food Programme. He hopes that through the development of this website, more people would become informed about the WFP's presence and their work, and as a result, more people would rally behind and contribute to the campaigns and advocacies supported by the WFP. Through this website, Dyll also hopes to raise greater awareness towards more individuals about global issues that are currently being faced by the entire human race. Among which are problems that stem and arise from hunger, conflict, injustice and inequality, and climate change. That through this raised awareness, more individuals would also begin to take action in addressing these problems and issues faced globally.
